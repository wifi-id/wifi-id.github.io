FAQ´s

  • 1

    I´ve bought an audio guide but I can´t see it in the “Purchased” section. Where can I find it and download it?

    In the upper right corner is the setup menu icon, you will recognize it by the three horizontal stripes. Press it and the menu will be displayed. Now choose “Purchased” and on the new screen choose “Restore Purchases”. Once you´ve done this you will be able to see all your purchases. On the right side you will see a Download button. Be sure to have a Wi-Fi connection at this point. Press the Download button so the audio guide can be saved to your device for you to enjoy whenever you want.

  • 2

    Browsing through Tupuy I´ve click on a destination which interests me but I want to listen to the first 2 tracks. I press on them but neither the image nor the audio are activated.

    This happens the first time you try to view a destination. This is because you´re device must download the content from our servers. It usually takes a few seconds, if it takes longer please wait. Once the process is done you will be able to see the image and the audio display button will be activated. Then press play.

  • 3

    How can I update Tupuy and see new audio guides?

    To update Tupuy you must drag down the search window until the update process is activated. After a few seconds all new audio guides will appear.

  • 4

    Why should I log in?

    Although you can enjoy TUPUY as an anonymous user, there are some reasons why we recommend you log in.

    1. In case you lose your device you will not be able to recover all the purchases since they are linked to your smartphone and not to a registered Tupuy user. However, if you are logged in every purchase will be linked to your account, no matter what device you use.

    2. As a Tupuy user you can be part of contests and great deals.

    We strongly recommend you register. It´s easy!

    Log in by Facebook or write your email and password. Done!

    Remember that if you change your device you should go to the "PURCHASED" configuration menu and restore your purchases. Don’t forget to download the audio guides after.
